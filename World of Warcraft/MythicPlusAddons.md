## Macros

Mouseover Macro e.g. Shaman's Chain Heal

```lua
#showtooltip
/cast [@mouseover,help,nodead][] Chain Heal
```

Cursor Macro e.g. Shaman's Healing Rain:

```lua
#showtooltip
/cast [@cursor] Healing Rain
```
